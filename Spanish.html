4 - 4 Latas

4 Latas is a 2019 Spanish comedy film directed by Gerardo Olivares and written by Olivares and Maria Jesus Petrement. The plot revolves around Tocho (Hovik Keuchkerian), who is a rough, bad-mannered alcoholic; Jean Pierre (Jean Reno), who was once a womanizer but has matured; and Ely (Susana Abaitua). They are all travelling from Spain to Ely’s father Joseba (Enrique San Francisco) who lives in Tombuctu, Mali, and is on his deathbed. They decided to travel there by using Ely's father’s old car, a car they used to do road trips on.

5 - I'm No Longer Here

In 2011, in the slums of Monterrey, Mexico, a 17 year-old teenager named Ulises is the leader of a gang called "Los Terkos". They dedicate themselves to hanging out and following the counter-culture of Kolombia [es]: a lifestyle that consists of dancing and listening to "Cumbia rebajada [es]" (a slowed version of Cumbia).
